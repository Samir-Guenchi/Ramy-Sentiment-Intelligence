Ramy Sentiment Intelligence — Understanding Customer Voice with AI
1 What We Built

Ramy Sentiment Intelligence is an AI-powered system that automatically reads and understands what customers say about Ramy products. It classifies every review into one of five categories:

Positive Negative Neutral Suggestion Question

Built specifically for the way Algerians actually write — mixing Arabic, Darija, and French in a single sentence — it achieves 95.06% accuracy with full transparency into why each decision was made.

2 The Challenge

Why is this hard? Traditional AI tools fail on Algerian text because customers mix three languages freely. Imagine a review like: "عصير Ramy بنين but الكارتون mauvais" — no off-the-shelf tool handles this reliably.

Our goals:

  • Build a 5-category classifier that truly works on real Algerian consumer voices
  • Guarantee honest metrics — no inflated scores from data leakage
  • Show why the AI made each decision (Explainable AI)
  • Deliver results through a live, real-time dashboard

Key technical choices:

  • AraBERT v2 — pre-trained on Maghrebi Arabic, ideal for Darija
  • Teacher-Student learning — the model teaches itself from unlabeled data
  • Leakage-free evaluation — test data never seen during training or tuning

5 What Makes This Special
  • 100% recall on Positive & Questions — never misses a satisfied customer or a purchase inquiry
  • Code-switching mastery — handles "بزاف bon" and "mauvais جدا" seamlessly
  • Honest scores — 3 leakage safeguards ensure real-world reliability

💡 Explainable AI (XAI) — Key Innovation

Our system doesn't just predict — it explains why. Using Attention Rollout and Gradient×Attention, it highlights which words drove each decision. For every prediction, stakeholders can see a color-coded evidence map showing the model's reasoning in real time.

📈 Business Value for Ramy

Actionable insights: Automatically detect product complaints, surface improvement suggestions, and identify purchase-intent questions across thousands of reviews. Enable data-driven decisions for product teams, marketing, and customer service — all in real time, at scale.
