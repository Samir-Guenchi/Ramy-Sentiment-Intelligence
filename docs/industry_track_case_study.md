# Industry Track Case Study: Ramy Sentiment Intelligence

## Track Fit Statement
This case study targets the Industry Track theme of applying sentiment analysis and opinion mining in real business environments. The system translates customer feedback into business intelligence for product evaluation, brand monitoring, and strategic decision-making.

## Business Context
Ramy receives customer opinions in Arabic, Algerian Darja, and mixed French text. Manual analysis does not scale and delays action. The platform automates sentiment understanding and transforms unstructured feedback into practical recommendations.

## Problem to Solve
1. Detect customer dissatisfaction early.
2. Understand which product aspects drive sentiment.
3. Track brand perception by product segment and geography.
4. Support marketing and product teams with evidence, not intuition.

## AI Solution Components
1. Sentiment classification for customer reviews and comments.
2. Aspect-based opinion mining for root-cause analysis.
3. Taxonomy-aware categorization of product families and subcategories.
4. Enterprise dashboard and APIs for filtering, trend analysis, and export.
5. Competition-grade training workflow with cross-validation and ensemble calibration.

## Enterprise Decision Workflow
1. Monitor sentiment trends daily.
2. Investigate anomalies by category, subcategory, and region.
3. Validate with review-level evidence.
4. Define corrective actions (formula, packaging, pricing, communication).
5. Track post-action sentiment change over time.

## KPIs for Industry Value
1. Negative sentiment detection latency.
2. Share of negative feedback by aspect.
3. Category-level sentiment improvement after interventions.
4. Regional recovery after campaign or product updates.
5. Analyst time saved via automation.

## Deployment Narrative for Jury
1. Show real-time dashboard intelligence.
2. Demonstrate drill-down from aggregate trend to specific reviews.
3. Present recommendation generated from evidence.
4. Explain measurable business impact and operational scalability.

## Conclusion
The project demonstrates how academic NLP innovation can be operationalized for enterprise outcomes. It aligns with the Industry Track objective of bridging AI research with practical business decision systems.
